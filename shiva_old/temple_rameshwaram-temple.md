# Rameshwaram Temple

## Location

Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India

## Description

A Jyotirlinga temple associated with the Ramayana, where Lord Rama is said to have worshipped Shiva before his exile.

## Significance

Symbolizes the connection between Shiva and the epic Ramayana; a site of pilgrimage for both Shaivites and Vaishnavites.

## Pilgrimage Info

Visited during the annual Ramanavami festival; pilgrims perform rituals to seek Shiva's blessings.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Rama
- Sita
