# Nataraja Temple

## Location

Chidambaram, Tamil Nadu, India

## Description

Houses the iconic bronze image of Shiva as Nataraja, the cosmic dancer. The temple's inner sanctum represents the 'Ananda Sabha' (Hall of Bliss).

## Significance

Embodies the philosophical concept of Shiva's dance of creation and destruction; a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

## Pilgrimage Info

Pilgrims visit to witness the 'Tandava' (dance) and participate in the annual 'Shivaratri' celebrations.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Ganesha
- Parvati
