# Shiva Jayanti

## Description

Celebrates the birth of Shiva. The festival emphasizes his role as the ultimate reality and the destroyer of evil.

## Rituals

- Early morning baths (snan) followed by worship of Shiva lingam
- Offering of fruits, flowers, and sweets
- Recitation of Shiva's mantras and stories
- Fasting and community feasts

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati

## Calendar

Third day of the bright half of Vaishakha (April-May)
