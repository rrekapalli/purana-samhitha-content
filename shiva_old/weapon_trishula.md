# Trishula

## History

The Trishula (trident) is one of Shiva's most iconic symbols, representing his role as the destroyer and transformer. It is depicted in his hands in numerous iconographic traditions, including the Shiva Purana and later Tantric texts. The three prongs symbolize the three realms (heaven, earth, and underworld), the three gunas (sattva, rajas, tamas), and the three aspects of time (past, present, future).

## Powers

- Destruction of evil
- Control over the three realms
- Symbol of cosmic balance

## Owners

- Shiva
- Sometimes depicted as wielded by other deities in art

## Associated Stories

- Used to defeat the demon Tripurasura in the Shiva Purana
- Symbolizes Shiva's supremacy over the universe
