# Damaru

## History

The damaru is a small drum associated with Shiva's role as the creator of the universe. According to the Shiva Purana, the sound of the damaru's beats is said to have given rise to the cosmos and the rhythm of existence. It is often depicted in Shiva's hands in artistic representations.

## Powers

- Creation of the universe
- Representation of cosmic rhythm
- Source of divine sound (Nada Brahma)

## Owners

- Shiva

## Associated Stories

- Shiva's damaru is said to have produced the first sound, leading to the birth of the universe
- Used in meditative practices to attain spiritual liberation
