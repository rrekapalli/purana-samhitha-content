# Mahakaleshwar Temple

## Location

Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, India

## Description

A major Jyotirlinga temple, with a lingam said to be self-manifested. The temple is associated with the Mahakal Fair.

## Significance

Considered the source of the Kshipra River; a site of immense spiritual power and historical importance.

## Pilgrimage Info

Pilgrims visit during the Mahakal Fair (July–August) and the Kumbh Mela.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
