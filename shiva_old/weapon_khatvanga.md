# Khatvanga

## History

The Khatvanga is a ritual staff with a skull, trident, and bell, associated with Shiva's role as a destroyer and ascetic. It appears in Tantric texts and later Hindu art, particularly in Nepal and India. The staff is linked to Shiva's ascetic practices and his ability to overcome death.

## Powers

- Destruction of evil
- Protection from negative forces
- Symbol of Shiva's transcendence over mortality

## Owners

- Shiva
- Used by Shiva's devotees in rituals

## Associated Stories

- Symbolizes Shiva's victory over death
- Used in Tantric rituals to channel divine energy
