# Kedarnath Temple

## Location

Kedarnath, Uttarakhand, India

## Description

A sacred Jyotirlinga shrine located in the Himalayas, believed to be Shiva's abode. The temple is part of the Char Dham pilgrimage circuit.

## Significance

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas; represents Shiva's cosmic form and is a site of intense spiritual significance.

## Pilgrimage Info

Accessible via a challenging trek from Gauri Kund; best visited during May–June. Pilgrims undertake the arduous journey to attain spiritual purification.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
