# Lingaraj Temple

## Location

Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India

## Description

A 11th-century temple dedicated to Shiva, showcasing Kalinga architecture. The deity is depicted as a lingam with a unique, towering structure.

## Significance

Symbolizes the triumph of good over evil; a major center of Shaivism in Odisha.

## Pilgrimage Info

Part of the Pancha Rathas complex; visited during the annual Mahashivratri festival.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
