# Kashi Vishwanath Temple

## Location

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India

## Description

Located on the banks of the Ganges, this temple is dedicated to Shiva as Vishwanath (Lord of the Universe).

## Significance

One of the oldest and most revered Shiva temples; Varanasi is considered the spiritual capital of India.

## Pilgrimage Info

Visited during Kumbh Mela; rituals here are believed to liberate souls from the cycle of rebirth.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Ganesha
- Parvati
