# Pashupatastra

## History

The Pashupatastra is a divine weapon created by Shiva, mentioned in the Mahabharata. It is described as an unstoppable weapon capable of annihilating entire armies. According to the Mahabharata, Shiva taught Arjuna the secrets of this weapon during his ascetic practices.

## Powers

- Unstoppable destruction
- Annihilation of entire armies
- Symbol of Shiva's ultimate power

## Owners

- Shiva
- Arjuna (in the Mahabharata)

## Associated Stories

- Used by Arjuna in the Mahabharata to destroy the Kaurava army
- Created by Shiva as a weapon of last resort
