# Third Eye

## History

Shiva's third eye, located on his forehead, is a symbol of his omniscience and destructive power. It is prominently featured in the Shiva Purana and other texts, particularly in the story of Shiva incinerating the god of love, Kama, for disturbing him during meditation.

## Powers

- Destruction of anything it gazes upon
- Omniscience
- Representation of spiritual awakening

## Owners

- Shiva

## Associated Stories

- Incinerated Kama (the god of love) when he disturbed Shiva's meditation
- Used to destroy the demon Tripurasura
