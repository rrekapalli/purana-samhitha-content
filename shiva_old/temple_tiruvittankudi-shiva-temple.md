# Tiruvittankudi Shiva Temple

## Location

Tiruvittankudi, Tamil Nadu, India

## Description

A temple associated with the story of Shiva's marriage to Parvati. The lingam is submerged in water, representing the union of Shiva and Parvati.

## Significance

Represents the divine union of Shiva and Parvati; a site of unique spiritual symbolism.

## Pilgrimage Info

Pilgrims visit to witness the submerged lingam and perform rituals during the annual festival.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
