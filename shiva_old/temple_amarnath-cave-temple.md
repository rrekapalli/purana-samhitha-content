# Amarnath Cave Temple

## Location

Amarnath, Jammu and Kashmir, India

## Description

A natural cave shrine housing a Shiva lingam formed by ice. The cave is believed to have been revealed to Parvati by Shiva.

## Significance

One of the holiest Shiva sites; the pilgrimage is considered a test of devotion and endurance.

## Pilgrimage Info

Undertaken during the Amarnath Yatra (July–August); involves a trek through the Pir Panjal Range.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
