# Kanyakumari Temple

## Location

Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, India

## Description

Dedicated to Shiva as Kanyakumari, a virgin girl. The temple is situated at the southernmost tip of the Indian peninsula.

## Significance

Symbolizes Shiva's protection of the earth; a site of pilgrimage for devotees seeking his blessings for marital and familial harmony.

## Pilgrimage Info

Visited by devotees during the annual Kanyakumari festival; the temple is a symbol of spiritual and geographical convergence.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
