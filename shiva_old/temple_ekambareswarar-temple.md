# Ekambareswarar Temple

## Location

Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu, India

## Description

One of the five major Shiva temples in Tamil Nadu, dedicated to Shiva as Ekambareswarar. The temple is surrounded by ancient rock-cut shrines.

## Significance

A center of Shaivism since the Pallava dynasty; the lingam is believed to be the 'first' among Shiva's manifestations.

## Pilgrimage Info

Visited during the annual Ekambareswarar festival; pilgrims perform abhishekam (ritual bathing) of the lingam.

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
