# Shiva Chaturthi

## Description

A festival dedicated to Shiva on the fourth day of the bright half of the month of Shravan (July-August). It commemorates Shiva's role as the destroyer and transformer.

## Rituals

- Offering of water (abhishekam) to the Shiva lingam
- Worship with flowers, incense, and lamps
- Recitation of Shiva's stories from the Shiva Purana
- Fasting and charitable acts

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati

## Calendar

Fourth day of the bright half of Shravan (July-August)
