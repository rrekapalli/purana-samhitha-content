# Maha Shivaratri

## Description

The Great Night of Shiva, celebrated as the night when Shiva performed the Tandava, the cosmic dance of creation and destruction. It is a time for fasting, meditation, and devotion to Shiva.

## Rituals

- Night-long vigils and chanting of Shiva's hymns (like the 'Shiva Panchakshari')
- Offering of milk, honey, and bilva leaves to the Shiva lingam
- Consumption of Bhang (cannabis) in some regions as a symbol of Shiva's intoxicating wisdom
- Fasting until sunrise or offering food to the poor after breaking the fast

## Associated Deities

- Shiva
- Parvati
- Ganesha

## Calendar

14th night of the new moon in the Hindu month of Phalgun (February-March)
