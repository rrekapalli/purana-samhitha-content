# Damru

## History

In the beginning, when the universe was silent, Lord Shiva plucked a small drum from the hollow of his own hand. This was the Damru, a tiny drum with two sides—one black, one white. When Shiva struck it, the sound echoed through the void, and the first breath of life entered the cosmos. The Damru’s rhythm became the heartbeat of the universe, its beats creating the cycles of time and the rise and fall of worlds. Once, when the demon Andhakasura blinded the gods with his darkness, Shiva played the Damru so loudly that the demon’s form shattered into smoke. The drum’s sound was both creation and destruction, a reminder that even silence holds power. To this day, the Damru rests in Shiva’s hand, its vibrations weaving the fabric of reality.

## Powers

- Creates the rhythm of time and space
- Destroys darkness with sound
- Awakens the soul’s inner music

## Owners

- Lord Shiva (the cosmic dancer)
- Nataraja (the king of dancers)
