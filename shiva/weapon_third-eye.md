# Third Eye

## History

Shiva’s third eye, hidden on his forehead, burned with the fire of divine knowledge. It was said to have been opened when the demon Tripurasura challenged the gods. With a single glance, Shiva’s third eye blazed so fiercely that the demon’s city crumbled into ashes. But the eye’s power was not only for destruction. Once, when a sage named Agastya asked Shiva for wisdom, the god closed his third eye, and the sage saw the entire universe within its depths. The eye could see beyond time, beyond illusion, beyond the veil of maya. It was a gift of truth, a tool to burn away ignorance. Even the gods feared its power, for it could reduce mountains to dust and turn oceans to steam. Yet Shiva used it only when the world needed a reminder of its own truth.

## Powers

- Destroys illusions and ignorance
- Sees the truth of all things
- Emits flames that can melt even the hardest hearts

## Owners

- Lord Shiva (the all-seeing god)
- Parvati (in some tales, she inherited its power)
