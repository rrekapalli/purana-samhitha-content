# Pashupatastra

## History

The Pashupatastra was a weapon so powerful that even the gods feared to wield it. It was said to have been created from Shiva’s own tears when he wept for the suffering of the world. The weapon could destroy anything—mountains, cities, even the universe itself. Only Shiva could command it, for it required the heart of a true ascetic to summon its power. Once, when the demon Mahishasura threatened to devour the gods, Shiva taught the warrior Arjuna the secret of the Pashupatastra. With a single arrow, Arjuna brought the demon to his knees. But Shiva warned that the weapon was not for pride or anger—it was for justice, for the protection of dharma. To use it without wisdom was to invite chaos.

## Powers

- Destroys anything, even the universe
- Can only be summoned by the purest of hearts
- Carries the weight of divine justice

## Owners

- Lord Shiva (the ultimate protector)
- Arjuna (the warrior of the Mahabharata)
