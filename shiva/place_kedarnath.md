# Kedarnath

## Type

REGION

## Description

Perched high in the snow-capped Himalayas, Kedarnath glows like a hidden jewel. The air hums with the sound of distant temple bells, and the ground shivers beneath the weight of ancient stones. A towering white temple, shaped like a lotus, stands on a cliffside, its golden roof catching the first light of dawn. The wind carries whispers of Shiva’s eternal dance, and the icy Mandakini River below mirrors the sky, as if the heavens have spilled into the earth.

## Significance

Shiva appeared here in a fierce form of a black bull, testing the devotion of the Pandavas. The temple’s stones are said to be his own skin, hardened by time and fire, and pilgrims believe that even a glance at the lingam inside can purify the soul.

## Geography

Nestled in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand, Kedarnath is cradled by the towering peaks of the Himalayas, with the Mandakini River carving a silver path through the valley below.
